About Us

With a stay at Skyline Hotel in Bogor City, you'll be just 5 minutes from Bogor Station. This 5-star hotel is located in the heart of Bogor's Central Business District. Make yourself at home in one of the 260 rooms equipped with a minibar and views of Bogor City from the corner. Complimentary wireless Internet access up to 250 Mbps keeps you connected, and satellite programming is available for your entertainment. Private bathroom with deep soaking tub and separate shower . Free toiletries and hairdryer. Conveniences include phones, as well as safes and desks. Pamper yourself with massages and facials on site. Guests can take vehicles to nearby destinations with a complimentary area shuttle. Enjoy international-class cuisine with fine dining at Skyline Restaurant & Lounge or stay in your room and take advantage of the 24-hour room service.
